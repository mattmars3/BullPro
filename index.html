Product

BullPro is a fully bulletproof clothing line designed to keep you safe in style. UHMWPE is an ultra-high-molecular-weight polyethylene, which is a chemical polymerization process in which monomers are converted to polymers. It is extremely strong compared to its weight, it has a high resistance to both ballistic, sharp edges, and pointed instruments. In addition to UHMWPE, we also use standard Kevlar.

Inspiration

41,000 Americans die from a gun every year. America is the number one country in the rankings of highest gun ownership. People around the United States have shown an increasing fear of gun violence. We decided that we would be the ones to do something about this and created BullPro.

Why BullPro

BullPro clothing allows you to have a strong sense of reliability and safety when you are wearing our clothing. Our clothing stands out from the rest of the market for many reasons. Our clothing is lightweight, discrete, comfortable, and most importantly, bulletproof.
